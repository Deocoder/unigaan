History of UNIGAAN

the UNITED GOSPEL ARTISTES ASSOCIATION OF NIGERIA was born in the year of our lord 2020, due to divisions found among the nigeria gospel artists which breached the unity of gospel artists in nigeria in pursing a common goal of propergating the gospel of our lord jesus chirst through music and the welfare of gospel artists, in promoting our individual talents and connecting to other nations of the world.these and more became a burden to some of our nigerian gospel music elites in the likes of apostle dr eugene mbaebie the founder of National association of gospel artistes nigeria (NAGA) now registered association of gospel artistes (AGAN) who also the vision bearer of UNITED GOSPEL ARTISTES ASSOCIATION OF NIGERIA (UNIGAAN) rev.dr emmanuel nnamdi ogbonna of the VOICE OF THE CROSS firm, rev.dr sam enyeribe nweze, evang. joel anosike and many more famous nigerian gospel artistes within and outside nigeria were called together by the vision bearer apostle eugene mbabie in the unity of purpose thus the UNITED GOSPEL ARTISTES ASSOCIATION OF NIGERIA (UNIGAAN) was formed to enhance and promote our god's given talents.

our goal

*to promote the spread of the gospel through Music
*to foster the spirit of friendliness,comradeship among gospel artistes
*to have a forum for enlightenment,teaching one another
*to help build and encourage upcoming artistes with different talents
*to help bring revivals in our individual churches and nations.
